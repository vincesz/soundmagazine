Don Toliver to release
'Life of a DON'

By Vince Szarka / 1Hr ago

Don Toliver via Spotify/Cactus Jack

It's finally here! Life of a DON is the second studio album and third official project by the American rapper and singer Don Toliver. Released October 8 following the success of his 2020 debut album, Heaven Or Hell, Don Toliver delivers both similar and new vibes on his new album.

The name of the album was hinted several times this summer by the rapper, but the official announcement was on September 28 on the rapper's Instagram. The 16-track album features people like Hip-Hop Superstar Travis Scott and new-found Rap artists like SoFaygo or HVN. Production done by the one and only MIKE DEAN and Don's long time partners like Dez Wright or Metro Boomin.

"My life your life."

stated by Don Toliver on his Instagram, he intends to talk about his personal life and let his fans listen to him as not Don Toliver, but as Caleb for the very first time. Tracks like Double Standards, Swangin' on Westheimer or Crossfaded are the perfect examples for this. Compared to Heaven or Hell we don't get as many hype songs but tracks like Flocky Flocky or Way Bigger definietly give back a similar but a more tuned down vibe.

The two singles leading up to the album's release were tracks What You Need and Drugs N Hella Melodies featuring Colombian-American singer Kali Uchis. On May 4, 2021 What You Need charted at #82 for a week on the Billboard Hot 100. However it is not the lead single of the album. In fact it's actually really hard to pick a lead song out of all the 16 tracks. Therefore it's safe to say that this thing is filled with bangers.

Overall the project was met with positive reviews by magazines like Pitchfork or fans on social media. Take a listen and let us know what do you think about the Houston rapper's new album!
