Where is Playboi Carti's
'Narcissist' ?

By Vince Szarka / 1Hr ago

Playboi Carti via Getty/Timothy Norris

The punk inspired Atlanta rapper Playboi Carti is back to his old self following the announcement of his new project 'Narcissist'. King Vamp (or Carti) announced a release date in late August on his Instagram but much to our surprise when the day came fans were left disappointed. So what happened? On August 23, 31 and September 12 Carti posted pictures on his Instagram showcasing somone wearing many layers of Balenciaga garments. "NARCISSIST 9/13/21" was the caption of the posts which left fans in anticipation for the new... well we don't know exactly what.

We were left with a word, a future date and a lot of confusion on what exactly is going to release or happen on this day. Until the 1st of September when some fans of Playboi Carti got an e-mail from Spotify saying that "Playboi Carti is giving you special access to get tickets to his upcoming tour!". According to Spotify only Carti's top fans got an e-mail like this.

So now we know that there is a tour from October to December. But as time goes by more and more details emerge on Twitter from people who work close with Carti. And according to them an album will allegedly drop on 13th of September. Or will it? Keep in mind that prior to this we had no idea of what this thing called "Narcissist" is going to be. No snippets, no teasers or even leaks could be found online about it. Fast forward and the 13th of september rolls around and 'Narcissist' is nowhere to be found. Atleast for the start of the day. Fans on Reddit and Twitter check Carti's website and see that it's been suddenly updated with new merchandise.

All of the goods featured the word Narcissist on them including hoodies, jackets and even a 5,000$ motorcylce helmet. But everything was shut down as fast as it appeared. Following all this Carti, for the first time in a while tweeted a picture. It was a text message where he says the following:

Follwed by a different tweet where he says "DROP NARCISSIST". A few days go by and pictures emerge of Carti and Lil Uzi Vert wearing unreleased black jackets with once again the word Narcissist. By this point most fans have already given up on hearing any new music from Carti. According to Playboi Carti's website the tour is still set to kick off on October 14 in Nashville, Tennessee.

On September 26, Carti tweets out the word: "Narcissist". Some billboards have also been spotted in San Antonio and Minneapolis the following week. Carti also tweeted some photos of the presumed Narcissist merch. On these pictures we can see the same jacket that Carti and Uzi were spotted wearing a few weeks ago in Los Angeles.

But that's as far as our story goes. With the tour starting today at 8PM in Nashville it's hard to say what will happen next or after the tour is done. Are we going to hear any unreleased songs during the concerts? Will King Vamp reveal any more information in the future? Well, so far we can only hope. Knowing the mysterious side of the rapper.
